Pre-trained Models

We provide pre-trained checkpoints for both stages of the model trained on CASME2, SAMM, and SMIC datasets.

Download pre-trained Checkpoints

You can download the pre-trained models checkpoints from Google Drive:

**Google Drive (checkpoints):** https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1pCcKMU3QvcM9APbDjV-zfgesc2sqyy2U?usp=sharing

Project source code is hosted on GitHub:

**GitHub repo:** https://github.com/ngtht71/megc

Checkpoint contents:

- `best_visual_encoder.pth` - Pre-trained CLIP visual encoder (Stage 1)
- `best_moe_megc.pth` - Trained Hierarchical MoE model (Stage 2)

Place downloaded `.pth` files in the `checkpoints/` directory before running training or inference.
